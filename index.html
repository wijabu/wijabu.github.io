I love to dig into customer problems and solve them with innovative technology.

Six years ago, when I started on the MedallionClass program with Princess Cruises, I saw something I couldn't look away from: Guest complaints took WAY too long to resolve.

But that was just the symptom. The root cause was a bloated, cumbersome process. So I made it my personal goal to streamline resolution of tickets.

I taught myself JavaScript. I started manipulating APIs to read and modify data. Then I really got cooking: I chained those APIs together via Postman to automate the whole process. That's when I discovered a passion for coding.

I am a creative problem solver with endless curiosity, and I still get a thrill seeing code I’ve written turn nothing into something. Reach out if you want to talk to me about my portfolio, new development opportunities or spicy takes on movies.
